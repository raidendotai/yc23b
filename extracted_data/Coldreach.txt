Hi! We are Shen and Hankel - the team behind Coldreach.
👪 Who is it for
Is your sales team:
- spending a lot of time writing sales emails?
- running templated email sequences that few people respond to?
- struggling to train good SDRs?
- finding it hard to write follow-up emails after meetings?
Then Coldreach is for you.
🧨 The Problem
Templated emails get no attention and land in spam, because so many people are spamming.
Personalization pays off, but it takes a lot of training to do it well, and takes >5 mins to research and write every email.
💡 Our solution
AI-powered templates
- instead of only replacing names on emails, Coldreach researches the prospect and fill in personalized information intelligently!
For example:
For every template, tweak the email for a few prospects manually, and the AI
learns from your tweaks to write the way you want
!
Import lead list from your favorite engagement platform or CRM easily, and start
sending personalized emails to everyone automatically
using your AI-powered templates.
Coldreach focuses on driving
scalable outbound sales
, but can also be applied on other forms of email interactions with prospects, regardless of inbound/outbound or cold/warm.
💪 Why we built Coldreach!
We built another startup also around LLMs, during which we wrote over 1,000 personalized sales emails for our outbound, so we know it converts, but hard to do at scale.
Thus, we decided to apply our expertise in writing sales emails and LLMs to solve this problem for other B2B companies struggling with it!
